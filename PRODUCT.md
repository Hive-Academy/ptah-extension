# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Professional software developers and engineering teams evaluating AI coding tools. They arrive at ptah.live from GitHub, the VS Code Marketplace, Reddit/Discord, or word of mouth — skeptical, tool-saturated, and quick to bounce. They already use Copilot or Claude Code and need a reason to believe Ptah is a tier above "another AI chat sidebar". Secondary audience: existing licensed users signing in for profile/pricing/downloads.

## Product Purpose

Ptah is an "AI coding orchestra": an orchestration platform delivered as a VS Code extension, an Electron desktop app, and a headless CLI, all sharing one core. It runs Claude, GitHub Copilot, OpenAI Codex, and Ollama through one harness, executes up to 9 agents in parallel on a canvas, and (via the Thoth suite) remembers across sessions, learns reusable skills, runs scheduled agents, and bridges to Telegram/Discord/Slack. The landing page's job: convert a skeptical developer into a 100-day trial install. Success = downloads and signups.

## Brand Personality

Sovereign, mystical, precise. "Ancient wisdom encoded in modern silicon" — Egyptian mythology (Ptah the creator-craftsman, Thoth the scribe who remembers) fused with terminal-native engineering credibility. Dark, dramatic, gold-on-obsidian. Power statements, never questions. Premium without being corporate; mythic without being a costume.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: cream backgrounds, identical icon-card grids, hero-metric templates, confetti gradients.
- "AI assistant" chat-widget framing — Ptah is an orchestration platform, never a chatbot.
- Crypto/web3 dark sites with neon-purple glows and fake 3D renders.
- Enterprise-beige developer-tool sites (boxy, lifeless, screenshot-dump pages).

## Design Principles

1. **Show the orchestra, not the chat** — every section proves parallelism, memory, or multi-runtime reach with real product surface, not abstract illustration.
2. **Myth carries the brand, engineering carries the claims** — Egyptian motifs set the atmosphere; every feature claim maps to shipped code.
3. **Motion is meaning** — scroll-driven sequences demonstrate how the product flows; decoration-only animation is cut.
4. **One gold** — the `#d4af37` spectrum is the only accent family; restraint makes the glow land.
5. **Every section earns a unique shape** — no two adjacent sections share the same layout skeleton.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. All text tokens verified ≥4.5:1 on Obsidian `#0a0a0a` (see DESIGN.md). `prefers-reduced-motion` honored globally via `@hive-academy/angular-gsap`; custom keyframes ship reduce-media overrides. Semantic sections with `aria-label`, decorative layers `aria-hidden`, focus-visible amber outlines on all interactive elements.
